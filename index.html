MarVin's Unique Tax Distribution

Smart Tax for a Noble Cause

MarVin’s tax distribution is designed to make a positive difference. With each transaction, 1% of the purchase tax is used to repurchase and burn tokens, reducing the total supply and enhancing value. Another 1% of the sales tax is dedicated to our stray dog rescue fund, supporting global initiatives to help dogs in need. This dual-purpose mechanism not only aims to boost the token’s value but also contributes to the welfare of stray animals.

  • Buy Tax 1% for token repurchase and burn.
  • Sell Tax: 1% allocated to stray dog rescue.
